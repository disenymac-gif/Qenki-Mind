# Meta-Model Investigation Log (Inconclusive Cases)

## Purpose
Records attempted refutations that ended without a conclusive result. An inconclusive experiment neither validates nor refutes the meta-model; it indicates a limitation in the experiment itself, not in the theory.

Examples of inconclusive causes:
- the artifact was incomplete
- two engines disagreed because one violated the protocol
- the artifact relied on a poorly documented external ontology
- translation between systems lost semantics
- the case depended on a defective implementation, not the meta-model

## Entry Format
- Artifact or case
- Level and dimension attempted (Validation Protocol level, Dataset Diversity category)
- Reason for inconclusiveness
- Whether the case should be retried after fixing the experimental issue
