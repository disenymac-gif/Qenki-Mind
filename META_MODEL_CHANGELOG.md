# Meta-Model Changelog (Churn Registry)

## Purpose
Tracks every change to META_MODEL.md caused by a confirmed Refutation Condition only. Successful validations that require no change are recorded separately in META_MODEL_VALIDATION.md. The ratio between the two logs (see Meta-Model Stability Index in META_MODEL_VALIDATION.md) is the primary empirical measure of evolutionary stability.

## Entry Format
Each entry must record:
- Artifact or case that triggered the change
- Refutation Condition violated
- Why no upper-layer extension solved the problem
- Which axiom, derived property, or rule was modified
