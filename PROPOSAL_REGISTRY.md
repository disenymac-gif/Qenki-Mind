# Proposal Registry (Cooling-Off Period)

## Purpose
This registry is not part of the standard and does not carry normative weight. It exists to prevent daily-use pressure from turning plausible improvement ideas into premature changes to the frozen standard (see FREEZE_DECLARATION.md).

## Rule
During an initial period (recommended: 6-12 months from the freeze date), any improvement idea must be registered here as a hypothesis, not implemented immediately, unless it already satisfies the Refutation Conditions in META_MODEL.md Section 6 or the evidentiary rules in VALIDATION_PROTOCOL.md.

Most proposals are expected to lose relevance once enough real evidence accumulates in META_MODEL_VALIDATION.md, META_MODEL_CHANGELOG.md, or META_MODEL_INVESTIGATION.md. A small number may persist and eventually justify a real evolution of the standard, at which point they graduate into a Confirmed Refutation or a documented Investigation.

## Entry Format
- Date proposed
- Idea (axiom, property, level, metric, or document proposed)
- Rationale
- Status: Open / Withdrawn / Superseded by evidence / Graduated to Refutation
