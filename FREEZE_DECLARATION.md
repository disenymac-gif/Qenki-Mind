## Modification Rule
- No conceptual change to any normative document without a Confirmed Refutation entry in META_MODEL_CHANGELOG.md.
- No methodological change to VALIDATION_PROTOCOL.md without a documented case in META_MODEL_INVESTIGATION.md or a Confirmed Refutation in META_MODEL_CHANGELOG.md.
- Any proposal lacking such an entry is archived, not debated. The burden of proof is on the proposal to change the standard, not on the standard to justify remaining unchanged.

## Definition of Success
Success is not the absence of refutation attempts. Success is sustaining a high rate of high-coverage, high-depth, high-breadth refutation attempts over years without frequent need to modify the standard. A standard nobody tries to break demonstrates nothing; a standard that survives hundreds of hostile attempts does.

## Effect
This declaration marks the official start of the Evidence Phase. From this point forward, the project shifts from optimizing the design to observing its behavior under real use across META_MODEL_VALIDATION.md, META_MODEL_CHANGELOG.md, and META_MODEL_INVESTIGATION.md.
